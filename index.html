Privacy Policy

CarLogging — Last updated: March 30, 2026

Introduction

CarLogging ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mileage and trip logging application designed for tax and expense tracking. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use our Android application.

Information We Collect

CarLogging collects the following types of data to provide its core functionality:

  • Location Data: We collect precise GPS location data to automatically detect and record your driving trips, including start and end points, routes, and distances traveled. Location data is collected only while the app is actively tracking a trip or running its background trip-detection service.
  • Trip and Mileage Data: Information you enter or that is automatically recorded about your trips, including dates, distances, purposes (business or personal), and any notes you add.
  • Vehicle Information: Details about your vehicles that you voluntarily enter, such as make, model, and year.

How We Use Your Information

All data collected by CarLogging is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Recording and displaying your trip history and mileage logs
  • Calculating distances and generating mileage reports for tax deduction purposes
  • Providing trip categorization (business vs. personal)

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your personal data, trip logs, or location history to any external servers. All data remains on your device under your control.

When data is in transit (for example, during device backup if enabled by your operating system), it is protected using industry-standard encryption protocols.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties. Your trip data, location history, and personal details are never shared with advertisers, analytics companies, or any other external parties.

Third-Party Services

CarLogging uses Google Maps SDK to display maps and calculate routes. Google's use of your data when interacting with maps is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We do not send your trip data to Google; only real-time map interactions are handled by the Maps SDK.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained on your device for as long as you use the app. You can delete individual trips or clear all data at any time from within the app's settings. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data from your device.

Children's Privacy

CarLogging is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Permissions

CarLogging requests the following device permissions:

  • Location (Fine/Coarse): Required for GPS-based trip tracking and distance calculation.
  • Background Location: Required for automatic trip detection while the app is not in the foreground.
  • Activity Recognition: Used to detect when you are driving to automatically start and stop trip recording.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
